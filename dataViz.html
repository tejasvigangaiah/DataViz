    • Selecting a Location

      Click on a bubble with city name to select it. Upon your selection it will be inflated to mark your selection.

      Selecting Cuisines

      You may select up to four cuisines at any given time. Your selections are inflated to highlight them. You may deselect them by clicking on the bubble again. Selecting/Deselecting a cuisine will update a venn diagram in the bottom section.

      Venn Diagram

      Venn Diagram is completely interactive element. Hovering over a region will highlight it and tool-tip will display the names of overlapping region. Clicking on the highlighted region will display recommended restaurants as nodes. on ven diagram.

      Recommendations

      As stated above, recommended restaurants are displayed on venn diagram inside your selected region. This nodes are also interactive, hovering over them will display a restaurant name. Statistics of the restaurant can be displayed by clicking on these nodes.

      Selecting Attributes

      Three selected attributes on index page are displayed as buttons at the bottom of the page. Initially, all three attributes are selected by default. User may select one attribute if desired.
